thumb|Bol, at the southern terminus the D115 road D115 is a state road on island of Brač in Croatia connecting the town of Bol and the D113 state road, which in turn connects the D115 road to Supetar from where Jadrolinija ferries float to the mainland, docking in Split and the D410 state road. The road is long.
