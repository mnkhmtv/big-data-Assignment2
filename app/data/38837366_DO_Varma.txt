D/O Varma is a 2013 Indian Telugu language film directed by Khaja. ==Cast== * Vennela Kishore as Rao Gopal Warma *Naveena Jackson as Deeksha *Roja as Ganga *Jogi Naidu as Rambabu *Dhanraj *Jeeva *Shankar Melkote ==Release== The movie was released on 15 May 2013. ==Reception== A critic from The Times of India wrote that "Director Khaja explores a subject that is not of the routine nature. It describes emotions that are more valuable than riches. Perhaps it is not something that can be called a popular subject but it sure can be described as meaningful cinema". ==References== Category:2013 films Category:2010s Telugu-language films
